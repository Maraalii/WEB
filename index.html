About Me

Who am I?

I am a passionate programmer and aspiring data scientist with a strong foundation in Business and Data Analysis, my journey in tech began with my curiosity about how websites made their interactive user interface; it ignited my curiosity and drive to explore more in this field, I delved into tech more in university, exploring computer science, I fell in love with business and settled on data science after my DSCI 100 class and my interest in hospital statistics that show women of color are more overlooked in the hospital, witnessing these disparities sparked a desire to use data analysis in more sectors of our society to uncover the disparities around us and use problem-solving and education to help people in these communities.

Currently, I'm expanding my skills through actively learning in personal projects and research. I am furthering my knowledge on SQL and Python and more. My goal is to leverage data and technology not just for innovation but to address current issues at their statistical core to educate the public and improve our way of living. When I'm not coding, you can find me reading, dancing, crocheting, or watching movies.
